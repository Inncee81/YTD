      ho neprozradím ani neřeknu, kde a jak ho získat.

      A Secure Token is required for proper function. Secure Token is a
      secret string, which is used to authentize the client to the server
      during the handshake. It's up to you to provide the token - YTD doesn't
      have it and I will not disclose either its value or how to get it, for
      legal reasons.
